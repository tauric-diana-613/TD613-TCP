# START_HERE


## First clicks

1. Open `app/index.html`.
2. Paste two text samples into the playground.
3. Toggle **mirror.off** and **badge.holds**.
4. Watch five things:
   - cadence similarity,
   - traceability,
   - recurrence pressure,
   - route pressure,
   - harbor recommendation.
5. Open the **engine panel** to see the branch / wave / harbor derivation.
