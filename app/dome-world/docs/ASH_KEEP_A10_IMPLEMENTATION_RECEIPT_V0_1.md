## 3. Residue grammar

A10 exposes five explicit and non-equivalent postures:

- **Shared** — both singleton readings already expose the same bounded relation;
- **Pair-emergent** — the relation appears only in combination;
- **Contradictory** — the readings remain in visible tension;
- **Missing** — a required source, route, comparison, or control is absent;
- **Unresolved** — available observations do not support lawful closure.

A local, editable human interpretation draft preserves the person’s authority to name significance. It creates no hidden scoring, learner inference, automatic storage, attribution, or verdict.
