# Ash Keep Five-Demo Rehydration Concordance v0.1

Status: **IMPLEMENTED / PR-GATED / PRODUCTION-PENDING**

This concordance records the diagnostic purpose and non-equivalence of the five synthetic Ash Keep demo profiles exercised through the child-legible AIA3 and Flow-Core interface.

## Shared purpose

The demos test one shared Ash engine without flattening every workspace into one decorative template. Each fixture must reveal a different class of navigation, hydration, lifecycle, route, disclosure, animation, or quiet-surface failure.

A hydrated demo may populate synthetic local structure and gesture-ready controls. It may not bypass lifecycle eligibility, create transport authority, authorize provider generation, infer passphrases, approve release, invent imported Reader output, or close human review.

## Non-equivalent diagnostic specimens

### Investigation

Route: **preserve source → map contradictions → test alternatives → human-review finding**

Primary stress: urgent inquiry without converting a difference into identity, intent, guilt, authorship, or truth.

### Political Campaign

Route: **confirm mandate → split public/private → route launch work → human-review claim**

Primary stress: fast launch work without leaking donor, host, targeting, security, coalition, or route-order joins.

### Fundraiser

Route: **state the gap → protect relationship joins → route asks and stewardship → human-review ask**

Primary stress: time-bound revenue work without inferring donor intent, payment status, guest identity, or conversion probability.

### Research Project

Route: **frame the question → inspect method and provenance → test and reproduce → human-review publication**

Primary stress: methods, nulls, ethics, consent, missingness, alternatives, and replication gaps without validity inflation or universal generalization.

### Legal Matter

Route: **verify deadline → preserve originals → separate privilege → test a bounded filing → human review**

Primary stress: deadlines, filings, evidence, confidentiality, privilege, competing explanations, and routes without legal advice, guilt, liability, merits, privilege waiver, or outcome prediction.

## Hydration ledger

Every profile must distinguish these states visibly and mechanically:

1. **Hydrated view** — the profile-specific workspace exists and can be opened.
2. **Gesture-ready** — an exact human control is available without implying that the action occurred.
3. **Lifecycle-held** — the control remains governed by Ash eligibility and prior receipts.
4. **Intentionally dormant** — provider output, release approval, invented novelty, imported Reader output, and passphrases remain quiet until their separate preconditions exist.
5. **Separate boundary** — destination handoff and external crossing remain outside local demo hydration.

- Prior Ash compositions remain veiled until the current ingress or open-case graph reaches coherent readiness.
- An open case cannot publish AIA3 readiness before lifecycle state, four routes, four task controls, visible membrane, exact work, and the active case pointer converge.
- Premium navigation opens the named workspace and may not force the page back to the obstructing top panel.

- Synthetic retired-worker registration and activation are separately bounded. Registration must resolve within 20 seconds; the resulting worker must reach `activated` within a further 20 seconds or fail with a named hold. Neither stage may wait indefinitely.
