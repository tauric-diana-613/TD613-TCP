## 0. Executive determination

This program begins with a concrete product failure.

Ash Keep’s custody architecture is exact enough to defend meaningful distinctions, yet its public route makes the custodian perform those distinctions as paperwork before the system has made their consequences legible. The most important example is Custody Root. A single surface currently concentrates source selection, local commitment, metadata posture, receipt registration, digest verification, Case Map ancestry, workspace admission, Rebuild freshness, derivative legitimacy, and continuity implications behind a narrow interaction.

The constitutional distinctions remain necessary:

```text
readiness ≠ custody
custody ≠ authenticity
case binding ≠ truth
rebuild eligibility ≠ release authority
continuity ≠ transport
```

The present interaction order nevertheless externalizes the system’s interpretive burden onto the operator. It asks the user to understand the ontology before the ontology has shown what it does.

The strategic goal is therefore:

> Transform Dome-World from an advanced instrument its authors can explain to a laboratory into a governed environment that makes its own operation legible through participation, consequence, naming, rest, and transfer—without requiring prior belief in TD613 ontology.

The Flow-Core Pedagogue Spine is not a tutorial, child mode, tooltip layer, help chatbot, or glyph-themed skin. It is a constitutional event engine that governs how consequential state transitions become perceptible, actionable, traceable, nameable, recoverable, and transferable.

Its canonical route is:

```text
NOTICE
→ ACT
→ WORLD ANSWERS
→ NAME
→ REST
→ TRANSFER
```

The environment answers before the ontology lectures. The glyph compresses a relation that has already been encountered. Rest remains a structural phase. Transfer tests whether the relation survives beyond the scene in which it first appeared.

The Information Dome—officially the first Dome of Dome-World—will be the low-risk proving environment for this engine. Ash Keep will remain the first high-stakes beneficiary.

## 8. Thermodynamic constitutional law

### 8.1 Separate heat from work

Thermal energy and mechanical work remain separate ledgers.

A heat engine remains bounded by:

\[
\eta_{Carnot}=1-\frac{T_c}{T_h}
\]

Low-grade solar heat may warm, dry, stabilize, or drive an appropriately modeled thermal cycle. It may not be spent as equivalent shaft work because a narrative requires water to move uphill.

### 8.2 Separate energy from exergy

Energy is conserved. Useful work potential degrades through irreversibility.

Friction converts organized mechanical energy into dispersed thermal energy. The joules remain; their availability changes.

Pedagogical language may say that a system “bleeds” in poetry. The physical ledger must say where exergy was destroyed and where energy moved.

### 8.3 Hydraulic efficiency

Micro-scale low-head storage must not inherit commercial pumped-hydro efficiency by wishful analogy. Planning values require a documented hardware and geometry basis. Until validated, the implementation must expose efficiency as an input range rather than a hidden constant.

### 8.4 No child-labor thermodynamics

A child’s play may create visible surplus:

- additional fountain height;
- ceremonial water;
- optional cooling reserve;
- faster loom motion;
- public spectacle.

It may not determine access to drinking water, sanitation, shelter, medical safety, or basic food preservation.

The world informs. It does not invoice the child for entropy.

## 15. Canonical Information Dome scenes

### 15.1 Scene A — Two rooms fail to glue

**Purpose:** teach local coherence, overlap, incompatibility, and repair without false consensus.

Route:

```text
NOTICE: two locally coherent rooms

WORLD ANSWERS: seam rises at the overlap
```

Acceptance:

- neither room is automatically declared false;
- receiving weights remain visible;
- mismatch source remains inspectable;
- alternate repair actions remain available;
- no global truth score appears.

### 15.2 Scene B — Same source, changed phason projection

**Purpose:** teach invariant content under changed admissible projection.

Route:

```text

TRANSFER: compare to Ash custody and register change
```

Acceptance:

- exact package decides the boundary where used;
- content digest remains fixed;
- projection change cannot imply source mutation;
- no identity or truth claim is emitted.

### 15.3 Scene C — Pair reveals what neither singleton shows

**Purpose:** teach relational emergence and experimental subtraction.

Route:

```text
NOTICE: baseline and two singleton views

WORLD ANSWERS: new bridge/node/chronology relation appears
```

Acceptance:

ACEDIT may perform controlled rendering and representational translation.

Pedagogue evaluation asks:

- did legibility increase?
- which relation became visible?
- which distinction disappeared?
- did source obligation survive?
- can a novice trace cause and effect?

Beauty does not validate the representation.

### 17.3 KIRA

KIRA guards the WORLD ANSWERS phase by asking whether different causes produce distinguishable observations.

Pedagogical questions:

- did the world answer distinctly enough?
- could two causes produce the same result?
- which additional action would separate them?
- what remains unobservable?

A world that answers every action identically teaches superstition rather than causality.
