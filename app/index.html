Quick lexicon

          
Short definitions for the dual-meaning labels on the deck. The fuller terminology map lives in docs/INTERFACE_LEXICON.md.

        

        

          

            
Mirror shield

            
Keeps public play exploratory. When armed, TCP will not treat high recognition as an open route.

          

          

            
Custody badge

            
A demo-state toggle that changes how strongly the deck leans toward branch-holding, buffering, or escalation language.

          

          

            
Harbor

            
The recommended passage function once recognition becomes strong enough to require structured handling.

          

          

            
Cadence shell

            
A reusable stylometric bias applied to a bay. The shell moves; the underlying text does not.

          

          

            
Effective archive

            
The side currently carrying custody in the model. Institutional means the archive is holding. Witness means continuity pressure has migrated outward.

          

          

            
Route pressure

            
The model's estimate of whether resemblance is staying decorative or starting to demand a path, receipt, and containment-aware response.
