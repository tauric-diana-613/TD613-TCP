Offline checklist

Read before relying on a packet

  1. Confirm the v2 SHI remains the public root.
  2. Confirm Phase 5 has not quarantined the packet.
  3. Confirm outside witnesses and Step 1 have not blocked release.
  4. Confirm Phase 8 public display mode before showing v3/SH3.
  5. Confirm Phase 9 release class before treating a packet as public-readable.
  6. Confirm release-facing artifacts report raw text as sealed.
  7. If Phase 8 or Phase 9 fields are absent, treat the capsule as stale and route back to the live Safe Harbor verifier.
