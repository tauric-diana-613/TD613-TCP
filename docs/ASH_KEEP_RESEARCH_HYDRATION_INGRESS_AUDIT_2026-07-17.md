## Ingress repair

The prior launch membrane used a fixed grid with `place-items:center` and no vertical overflow contract. That posture works only when the panel is smaller than the viewport. On short desktop viewports, browser zoom, font expansion, injected status copy, or platform chrome can push actions below the reachable area.

The repaired membrane uses:

- a fixed full-viewport flex membrane;
- stable horizontal centering;
- `overflow-y:auto` on both membrane and panel boundaries;
- `max-height` derived from `100dvh`, with `100vh` fallback;
- stable scrollbar gutters;
- centered `margin:auto` while the panel fits;
- top-reachable scroll behavior when the panel exceeds the viewport;
- no horizontal overflow authority.

```text
centered when fitting
≠
forced centered clipping when oversized
```
