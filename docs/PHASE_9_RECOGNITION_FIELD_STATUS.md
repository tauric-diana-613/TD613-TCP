# Phase 9 Status — Recognition Field Simulator

Phase 9 adds local context-pressure simulation to TD613-TCP.

Recognition Field simulation estimates how protected output may behave across communication contexts such as group chat, forum post, public comment, legal intake, HR/compliance portal, protected tip form, internal workspace, and document handoff.

It measures:

- indexability
- topic leakage
- entity leakage
- Persona continuity pressure
- mask overuse pressure
- context legibility
- recognition recapture pressure

Recognition Field simulation does not know hidden platform classifiers and does not predict platform outcomes.

It is a local pressure surface that helps operators decide whether to continue, hold, rotate, restore, compress, or seal.

## Local-pressure posture

Recognition Field is advisory pressure, not evidentiary permission. It can lower practical route posture, add limitations, and recommend controller hints. It cannot raise Claim Ladder authority into platform certainty or identity proof.

## Contexts included

- Group Chat
- Forum Post
- Public Comment
- Legal Intake
- HR / Compliance Portal
- Protected Tip Form
- Internal Workspace
- Document Handoff

## Report export

Phase 9 extends local report payloads with an optional Recognition Field summary. Reports still exclude private text by default and keep forbidden conclusions blocked.
