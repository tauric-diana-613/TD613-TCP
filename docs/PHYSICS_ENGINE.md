# PHYSICS ENGINE

The engine does not claim that social, archival, or custodial systems are literally reducible to mechanics or quantum theory. It uses three disciplined structural analogies to model how patterned signal appears, becomes legible, and either routes into passage or collapses into criticality.

## 1. Branch engine

From the quadratic lesson:

```math
t^2 - 2t - 3 = 0 \Rightarrow t \in \{3,-1\}
```

The negative root is not treated as disposable simply because it fails the immediate scenario. It is preserved as a **candidate discovery branch**: a surplus generated by the model that may disclose a missing frame, earlier state, or deeper order.
