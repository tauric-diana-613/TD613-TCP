# Rupture and the Toni Clause

## Rupture

Rupture is the visible failure of smooth closure: when a score, report, Persona, context, or output cannot honestly collapse into a clean conclusion.

In TD613 Hush, rupture is not a bug to hide. It is a signal that review must slow down.

## Rupture in Hush

Rupture appears when:

- semantic fidelity drops
- protected literals disappear
- source residual remains elevated
- ingestion friction becomes unstable
- Persona continuity becomes overfit
- Recognition Field pressure rises
- the controller holds, restores, or rotates
- the Claim Ladder lowers the permitted conclusion
- fixtures fail in uncomfortable but informative ways

The tool honors rupture by keeping the break visible.

## The Toni clause

> “If you have freedom, you must free someone else. If you have power, your job is to empower somebody else.”
> — Toni Morrison

The Toni clause means Hush cannot only protect the builder. It must become usable by the next operator, including the operator with fewer resources, less institutional protection, less time, and more retaliation risk.

## Whistleblower translation

Whistleblowers need more than a pretty rewrite. They need evidence preserved, private text protected by default, claim language disciplined, and uncertainty made visible before exposure.

Hush empowers by refusing false certainty, preserving private text by default, warning when evidence is thin, and making hostile contexts visible before vulnerable speakers mistake local convergence for safety.

## Design commitments

- Preserve limits in reports.
- Keep refusal behavior visible.
- Treat private text as custody-sensitive.
- Separate topic pressure from authorship pressure.
- Separate entity preservation from exposure pressure.
- Keep Recognition Field advisory.
- Keep Claim Ladder as the ceiling on language.
- Keep Hush distinct from TD613 Flight and Safe Harbor.

## What empowerment requires

Empowerment requires the tool to tell the truth about its own reach. Hush should help operators see pressure, not seduce them into certainty.

The tool honors rupture by keeping the break visible. It honors the Toni clause by turning that visibility into shared operator power.
