# Phase 13 — Profile Fidelity Acceptance

Phase 13 passes when candidate selection can reject smooth generic prose and prefer a rougher candidate that better satisfies the selected mask profile.

Acceptance requires profile data, smoothness penalty, mask-native variance scoring, semantic blockers, line and paragraph topology scoring, selector rationale, and non-claims.

Phase 13 may claim observed profile fidelity. It may not claim authorship proof, identity proof, anonymity, human equivalence, legal safety, or perfect voice match.
