## Diagnosis

The mask is not a sloppy-writing transform, a burnout diagnosis, a medical-state inference, or a grammar-degradation filter.

The mask is a quick handoff custody layer. It permits light punctuation, line breaks, caps artifacts, and low ornament only when required source obligations survive.
