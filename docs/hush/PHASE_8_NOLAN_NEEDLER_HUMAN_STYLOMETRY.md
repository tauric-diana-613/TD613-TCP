# PHASE 8.N — Nolan the Needler Human-Stylometry Gate

Nolan the Needler is the low-heat edge gate for `soft-snark`.

His purpose is one controlled tonal perturbation followed by immediate receipt custody. The gate measures low-heat edge, receipt-return latency, escalation boundaries, reusable quip risk, and cross-output style stability.

## Core rule

One eyebrow. Then the receipt.

Operational rule:

One perturbation. Immediate anchor. No residue.

## Native route

`soft-snark` routes to the `low_heat_edge` role.

The passport uses a native centroid with variance requirements. The candidate must keep receipt custody high while preventing the dry turn from becoming a reusable stylistic pattern.

## Cadence heatmap

Expected contour: `one-perturbation-immediate-receipt`.

- Perturbation: 0.00–0.20
- Anchor: 0.20–0.45
- Relation: 0.45–0.75
- Closure: 0.75–1.00

The contour must differ from stacked snark, target-register replacement, fracture, warm receipt, cold ledger, and generic memo surfaces.
