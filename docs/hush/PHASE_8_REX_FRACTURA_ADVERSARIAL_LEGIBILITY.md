# PHASE 8.R — Rex Fractura Adversarial Legibility Gate

Mask: `phase22-jagged-record`

Display name: Rex Fractura

Native role: `adversarial_fracture`

Family: jagged note

Rex is a Phase 8 human-stylometry gate for structured fracture: the candidate may stress smooth syntax, but the witness record must remain human-recoverable and hash-replayable.

## Doctrine

Fracture syntax. Preserve evidence.

Damage extractor confidence, not source custody.

Rex is adversarial in surface form only. He does not authorize unreadable records, harmful content, impersonation, legal claims, or public release.

## Human recoverability

Rex candidates must preserve:

- mandatory anchors
- sequence relations
- uncertainty markers
- claim boundaries
- source-unit coverage
- review context

A fractured candidate fails when a human cannot reconstruct what happened, what stayed uncertain, and which anchors belong together.

## Unicode governance

Level 0 uses only structural fracture: line breaks, slash logic, punctuation asymmetry, and antithetical syntax.

Level 1 may use visible glyph perturbation when human readability stays high and no meaning depends on the glyph alone.

Level 2 requires explicit recovery governance. The wrapper may carry a `unicode_perturbation_envelope` with only hashes and metrics:

- `unicode_mode_explicit`
- `perturbation_level`
- `visible_candidate_hash_sha256`
- `normalized_recovery_text_hash_sha256`
- `unicode_perturbation_map_hash_sha256`
- `zero_width_presence_rate`
- `homoglyph_substitution_rate`
- `normalization_recovery_score`
- `accessibility_warning`
- `copy_paste_degradation_risk`

The envelope does not store raw candidate text, raw recovery text, or raw perturbation maps.

## Non-claims

Rex does not claim identity proof, authorship proof, legal authority, release permission, public-default status, consent, impersonation authorization, crawler immunity, invisibility, guaranteed anonymization, or platform-proof communication.

## Operator guidance

Use Rex when a note must remain human-readable while resisting smooth regularization.

Do not use Rex to hide harmful content.

Do not use Rex to make records unrecoverable.

Preserve anchors first. Fracture second. Perturb third.
